# Master Reservations Checklist

Single source of truth for everything that needs to be booked, reserved, or mailed for the Oct 23 – Nov 6, 2026 Japan trip. **Sorted by deadline, not by category.** Tick boxes provided so this file doubles as a working checklist.

Today's date: **2026-05-17.** Trip dates: **Oct 23 – Nov 6, 2026.**

## Critical path

Three items will break the trip if late: (1) the **Hakone Suimeisou** balcony-onsen room booking (only 12 of those rooms exist, sell first); (2) the **Saiho-ji postcard** mailed in early September with International Reply Coupons — without the temple's return postcard you do not get in on Nov 5; (3) the **Nōhi Bus Takayama→Shirakawa-go 07:50 on Nov 1**, a foliage Sunday, reservation window opens Oct 1 09:00 JST and sells out within hours. Everything else has slack or a workable substitute.

## Right now — this week (May 17–24, 2026)

The three city accommodations + the Hakone ryokan are all "book this week." Sat Oct 31 in Takayama is the hardest single night in the entire trip; Suimeisou's private-balcony-onsen tier is already eroding; Hotel Graphy Nezu's 5-night Oct twin availability is partially gone per `hotels-tokyo.md`.

- [ ] **Hotel Graphy Nezu Tokyo — 5 nights Oct 23–27** — book via [Booking.com](https://www.booking.com/hotel/jp/graphy-nezu.html). **Deluxe Twin with private bath** (~€85–105/night, ~€425–525 total). ## Saiho-ji postcard contents

Mail in **early September 2026** (Sep 5–10 ideal). Address details verified May 2026 via saihoji-kokedera.com/postcard/en and intosaihoji.com.

- Home address (group representative): [Tallinn address]
- Phone / email: [phone] / clemens.a.janes@gmail.com
- Number of visitors: **2**
- Preferred visit date: **November 5, 2026** (1st choice). Alternates: **November 4, 2026; November 6, 2026.**

## What this list deliberately does NOT include

- **JR Pass.** Not used on this trip — individual tickets save ~€200 pp. See `budget.md`.
- **Alcohol pairings, sake brewery tours, Kibune drinking experiences.** Stripped per trip rules. The kaiseki bookings explicitly request hojicha / sencha / mugicha tea service instead.
- **Saiho-ji online booking (intosaihoji.com)** — only the fallback if the postcard misses the Oct 28 cutoff. Use postcard as primary.
- **Pre-trip eSIM activation.** The eSIM activates on arrival; buy it any time in summer.
- **Restaurants without reservations** (Tsukiji breakfast crawl, Suzuya, Tonkatsu Maisen Aoyama, Nishiki Market stalls, etc.) — these are walk-in by design and don't belong on a deadline list.
