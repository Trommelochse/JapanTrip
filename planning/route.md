# Locked Route

## Shape

**Tokyo (5n) → Hakone (2n) → Takayama (3n, w/ Shirakawa-go day) → Kyoto (4n) → fly out KIX**

14 nights / 15 days. **Tokyo-first.** HND in, KIX out.

> Note: this corrects an earlier off-by-one in the shape header — the day table below was always authoritative. 5+2+3+4 = 14 nights. Takayama gets 3 nights because Shirakawa-go is a full-day trip that warrants its own sleep at both ends; Kyoto gets 4 nights (Day 11 arrival evening + Days 12–14, with Day 15 morning → KIX).

## Dates

**Oct 23 – Nov 6, 2026** — LOCKED.

Strategist rationale:
- Threads between Oct 31–Nov 3 Culture Day surge and Nov 21–23 Labor Thanksgiving Kyoto disaster.
- Takayama + Shirakawa-go land **Nov 1–2 at alpine peak foliage** — the most reliable foliage payoff in the trip.
- Hakone Oct 28–29: early color, not peak; the splurge is the ryokan experience, not the gardens.
- Kyoto Nov 2–6: green-to-early-yellow; trade accepted for sane crowd levels.
- Tokyo-first absorbs jetlag where it doesn't hurt.
